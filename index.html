Principal Physics Formulas

Topological Entropy ($S_{topo}$)

$$S_{topo} = - \sum p_i \ln p_i$$

Measures the informational disorder within the interaction matrix.

Interaction Curvature ($\kappa$)

$$\kappa = \frac{|\vec{N}_i - \vec{N}_{i-1}|}{d}$$

Quantifies the bending of the property space between successive molecules.

Stability Index ($\Phi$)

$$\Phi = \frac{N_z}{\sqrt{N_x^2 + N_y^2}}$$

Ratio of the vertical stable ridge to the lateral topological resistance.
